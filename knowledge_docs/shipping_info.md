# Shipping Information

## Delivery Times

### Standard Shipping (Free on orders over $50)
- Delivery: 5-7 business days
- Available for all US addresses
- Tracking provided via email

### Express Shipping ($9.99)
- Delivery: 2-3 business days
- Available for all US addresses
- Full tracking with SMS updates

### Overnight Shipping ($19.99)
- Delivery: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST)
- Available for most US addresses (some rural areas excluded)
- Signature required on delivery

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same day (Monday-Friday)
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends ship next business day
- Processing time may extend to 2-3 days during peak seasons (Black Friday, holidays)

## Tracking Your Order
- Tracking number sent via email once order ships
- Track online at our website or carrier's site (USPS, UPS, FedEx)
- Real-time updates on package location
- Delivery confirmation sent upon successful delivery

## International Shipping
- Available to Canada, UK, EU, and Australia
- Delivery: 10-14 business days
- Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient
- International shipping cost calculated at checkout based on weight and destination

## Shipping Issues

### Package Not Received
- Check tracking status for latest updates
- Allow 1-2 extra days for carrier delays
- Contact us if package shows delivered but not received - we'll file a claim

### Damaged Package
- Take photos of the damaged packaging and product
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- We'll send a replacement or issue a refund at no extra cost

### Wrong Address
- Contact us immediately if you entered the wrong address
- If package hasn't shipped, we can update the address
- If already shipped, we may be able to intercept (additional fee may apply)

## PO Boxes and APO/FPO
- Standard shipping available to PO Boxes
- Express and overnight shipping not available for PO Boxes
- APO/FPO addresses supported with standard shipping (7-14 days)
