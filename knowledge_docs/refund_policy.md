# Refund Policy

## 30-Day Defect Returns
- Products with manufacturing defects can be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Defects include: malfunctioning components, broken parts on arrival, or items not working as advertised
- Full refund or replacement is offered at customer's choice
- Original receipt or order confirmation required

## 14-Day Buyer's Remorse Returns
- Changed your mind? Return any unused item within 14 days of delivery
- Items must be in original packaging, unopened and unused
- A 10% restocking fee applies to buyer's remorse returns
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days after item received

## Warranty Information
- All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship
- Warranty does not cover: accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized modifications
- Extended warranty available for purchase (2-year or 3-year options)

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Contact customer support with your order number
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
3. Ship the item back using the provided prepaid label (for defects) or at your own cost (buyer's remorse)
4. Refund processed upon inspection of returned item

## Non-Refundable Items
- Opened software or digital downloads
- Gift cards
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Items marked as "Final Sale"

## Exchanges
- Exchanges are processed as returns plus new orders
- Size or color exchanges can be expedited if stock is available
- Contact support within 14 days to arrange an exchange
