# Product Troubleshooting Guide

## Wireless Headphones

### Headphones Not Charging
1. **Check the charging cable**: Try a different USB cable
2. **Clean the charging port**: Use a soft brush to remove debris
3. **Check power source**: Try a different USB port or wall adapter
4. **Reset the headphones**: Hold the power button for 15 seconds
5. **Battery indicator**: If no lights show after 30 minutes of charging, battery may be defective

### Headphones Not Turning On
1. Ensure headphones are charged (charge for at least 30 minutes)
2. Perform a hard reset: Hold power button for 20 seconds
3. Check if headphones are in pairing mode (flashing lights)
4. If still unresponsive, contact support for warranty replacement

### Bluetooth Connection Issues
1. **Forget device**: Remove headphones from phone's Bluetooth settings
2. **Reset headphones**: Hold power button + volume down for 10 seconds
3. **Re-pair**: Put headphones in pairing mode (hold power 5 seconds until blue light flashes)
4. **Check range**: Stay within 30 feet of connected device
5. **Interference**: Move away from WiFi routers and microwaves

### Audio Quality Problems
- **Crackling/static**: Update headphone firmware via companion app
- **Low volume**: Check phone volume and headphone volume separately
- **One side quieter**: Clean ear cushions and speaker mesh
- **No bass**: Ensure proper fit; try different ear tips if included

## Laptop Stand

### Stand Wobbles or Unstable
1. Check all screws are tightened properly
2. Ensure surface is flat and level
3. Adjust angle setting - some positions may be less stable
4. Check rubber feet are intact and not worn

### Height Adjustment Not Working
1. Locate the adjustment lever/button
2. Press firmly while adjusting height
3. Clean adjustment mechanism with dry cloth
4. Do not exceed weight limit (typically 15 lbs)

## USB-C Hub

### Devices Not Recognized
1. Try different USB-C port on your laptop
2. Check if hub requires external power
3. Update laptop drivers/firmware
4. Try connecting devices one at a time

### Overheating
1. Ensure adequate ventilation around hub
2. Disconnect unused devices
3. Avoid covering the hub
4. If severely hot, disconnect and contact support

## Mechanical Keyboard

### Keys Not Working
1. Check USB connection
2. Try different USB port
3. Update keyboard drivers
4. For specific keys: remove keycap and clean with compressed air

### Double Typing (Chattering)
1. Update keyboard firmware
2. Clean affected switch with contact cleaner
3. Adjust debounce settings if available in software
4. May indicate switch wear - contact support for warranty

### Backlight Issues
1. Check software settings for lighting modes
2. Ensure keyboard software is installed
3. Try different USB port (some ports don't supply enough power)
